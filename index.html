About

From accounting practice to education inquiry

I am Li Sining, an accounting undergraduate from Jilin University of Finance and Economics preparing for graduate study in Educational Studies at The Education University of Hong Kong. My academic direction is shaped by a practical background in auditing and tax-related work, where I developed attention to evidence, institutional processes, and evaluation.

My current interest lies in understanding how higher education systems support students, assess quality, and respond to policy change. I hope to develop a rigorous foundation for doctoral study in education and long-term academic career development.
